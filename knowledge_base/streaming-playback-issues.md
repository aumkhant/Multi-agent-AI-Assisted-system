# Fixing streaming playback issues

If a title buffers, stalls, or won't play:

1. Confirm the title shows "Available for streaming" in the catalog — some titles are
   rental-only and are not streamable.
2. Check your internet connection is at least 5 Mbps for standard definition.
3. Restart the app or refresh the browser tab.
4. Sign out and back in if playback controls are unresponsive.

If the issue persists after these steps, it should be escalated to a human agent.
