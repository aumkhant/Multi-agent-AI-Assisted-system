# Updating your payment method

To update your payment method:

1. Go to Account Settings > Billing.
2. Select "Payment Methods".
3. Click "Add new card" and enter your card details.
4. Set the new card as default, then remove the old one if you no longer want it.

Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If a payment fails, we will retry twice
over 3 days before pausing your subscription.
